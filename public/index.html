About SHARE BOOST

SHARE BOOST is a powerful automation tool designed to help you increase the visibility of your Facebook posts through automated sharing. It manages multiple sessions and provides real-time progress tracking.

How to Use

1
Get Facebook Cookies Extract your cookies from a logged-in Facebook session using a browser extension.
2
Enter Post Details Paste the full URL of the Facebook post you want to boost.
3
Configure Settings Set the total amount of shares and the interval (in seconds) between each share.
4
Start Boosting Click "Start Sharing" and monitor progress in the Home or Sessions tab.
Important Tip

Use longer intervals (30s+) and moderate share amounts to keep your account safe from Facebook's automated detection.
